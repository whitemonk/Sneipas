About the Studio

We make things worth being made.

Sneipas, spelled backwards from Sapiens, is an independent creative studio based in Sweden. It chooses to walk in the opposite direction of "wisdom," "progress," “forward".

Working with the intersection of texts, visuals and sounds, it aims to produce works that carries weight without over-production.

The idea is seeing a new world through old lens, making things worth remembering and reflections.
